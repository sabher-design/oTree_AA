During the tournament, you will complete the word encoding task for 5 minutes. Your earnings from this task will depend on what we call “final performance”. It consists of the number of correctly encoded words times a multiplier. Female participants have a multiplier of 0.9. Male participants have a multiplier of 1.

For example, if you are a woman and you encode 10 words correctly, your final performance is 10 x 0.9 = 9 words. If you are a man and you encode 10 words correctly, your final performance is 10 x 1 = 10 words.
